Our Specialities:-


Cancer Care and Treatment :-
We believe in treating Cancer with a combination of Chemotherapy, Radiation Therapy, Surgery and Targeted Therapy. We are the first facility in northern India to acquire Novalis Tx for IMRT/IGRT, Surgery, HIPEC and SRS/SRT. Additionally, we are equipped with an advanced Da Vinci XI Robotic System for treating complex conditions like cancers of prostate, cervix, colon/rectum, as well as heart tumors.


Cardiac Sciences
We are a tertiary care centre equipped with cutting edge technology to offer cardiac care program to our patients. Our hospital is equipped with state of the art Cath Labs, operation theatres, and several other heart care technologies.


Gastroenterology
